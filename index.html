RESOURCE CENTER

GST Registration — Document Checklist

All the documents you need for a hassle-free GST registration. We guide you through every step.

👥 Common Documents (All Business Types)

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Mobile Number & Email ID

🏠 Principal Place of Business Proof

  • Owned: Electricity Bill / Property Tax Receipt
  • Rented: Rent Agreement + Electricity Bill
  • NOC from Owner (if applicable)

🏭 Company / Pvt Ltd / Public Ltd

  • PAN of Company
  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • MOA & AOA
  • Board Resolution / Authorisation Letter
  • ID & Address Proof of Directors

🏦 LLP / Trust / Society / NGO

  • PAN of LLP / Trust / Society
  • LLP Agreement / Trust Deed / Bye-laws
  • Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
  • ID & Address Proof of Partners / Trustees

Not sure what documents you need? Our experts will guide you through the entire process — fast, accurate, stress-free.
